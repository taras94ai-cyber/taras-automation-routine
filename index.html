How it works

Three steps. No new habits.

It works in the Telegram group chat that your team already uses. No special skills or apps.

01

Take a picture

The person who makes the withdrawals and receives the check or invoice takes a picture (or automatically scans it from your email), or writes the amount in text, or speaks in voice into the team chat — as usual.

02

Self-Reading

Information about the supplier, date, amount and category is automatically extracted from the receipt in a few seconds.

03

It's archived.

Sorted by task, backed up, and ready to search or share with your accountant — no printing required.
